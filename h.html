Hypophosphatasia
A diagnosis may be confirmed by a thorough clinical evaluation, a detail patient history (including family history) and specialized tests that reveal absence or deficient activity of the enzyme lysosomal lipase acid (LIPA) in certain cells and tissues of the body. Molecular genetic testing for mutations in the LIPA gene is also available.
